About

My education and work experience.

  • 2002-2006

    Illustration - Communications Design

    I came to Brooklyn from Maryland to expand my background in painting and drawing. In addition to those skills, I learned the Adobe Creative Suite, photography, video editing and even some music.

  • September - December 2006

    Graphic Designer

    At this consulting firm, I was taken on as a paid intern during their college recruitment period. I designed print and web posters, flyers and banners that were distributed at Ivy League schools.

  • 2007-2011

    Web Designer

    My first opportunities to manipulate HTML and CSS came from updating and rebranding web site templates. I was able to use my design background to design banners and navigational elements.

  • 2011 - Present

    Web Developer

    Expanding on my previous skills, I have had the opportunity to build custom responsive web pages using HTML5, CSS3, Javascript. I am actively involved in the entire process from wireframing, to back end development, testing and publishing.
